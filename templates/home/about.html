Goodmil Construction's Story

Goodmil Construction has been a well-established name in the construction industry since its inception in 2013 by Amrit Tamang, who possesses more than 25 years of experience in the relevant field. Tamang continues to head the company and has played a significant role in establishing it as a leading performer, known for delivering excellent work and exceptional customer service.

The company's success is a result of its commitment to excellence in every aspect of its work. From the initial design phase to the final touches on a project, Goodmil Construction's team of experts is dedicated to providing the best possible service to its clients. With a focus on innovation and the latest technologies, the company is always on the cutting edge of the industry, ensuring that its projects are not only of the highest quality, but also efficient and sustainable.

Over the years, Goodmil Construction has worked on a wide range of projects, from small residential renovations to large commercial developments. Despite its success, the company remains committed to its core values of honesty, integrity, and hard work. These values have helped to build a loyal customer base and a team of dedicated employees who are passionate about what they do. With a commitment to excellence and a focus on innovation, Goodmil Construction is poised for continued success in the years to come.The company demonstrated its dedication to aiding the community in times of crisis by significantly contributing to the restoration and improvement of various vital structures, such as schools, hospitals, and other buildings that were devastated by the 2015 earthquake in Nepal.
