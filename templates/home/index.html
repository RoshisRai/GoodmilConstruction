Goodmil Construction's Story

Goodmil Amrit Nirman Sewa (Goodmil Construction) has a long-standing presence in the construction industry, tracing back to its establishment in 2013 by Amrit Tamang, who has over 25 years of experience in the field. Tamang is still in charge of the company and has led it to rise to prominence as a top performer, renowned for its high-quality output and outstanding customer service.

The company's success is a result of its commitment to excellence in every aspect of its work. From the initial design phase to the final touches on a project, Goodmil Construction's team of experts is dedicated to providing the best possible service to its clients. The company also played a huge role in repairing and renovating the buildings destroyed by the earthquake that hit Nepal in 2015, including schools, hospitals, and other important structures, demonstrating its commitment to contributing to the community in times of need.
